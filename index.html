Working Group on Differential Cohesive Topos

Outline

The main goal of this seminar is to understand Urs Schreiber’s work Differential cohomology in a cohesive infinity-topos and other related material.

Format

This seminar is inspired Exodromy. We aim for informal talks, and we can take as much time as we need. This group can last for a long period so that we can have time to work on more content.

Talks will be on sometime each week (TBA) through Zoom. Everyone who wants to participate can fill the form below and join our Discord. You can also contact me by email
